The Challenge

The Problem

Infrastructure in India suffers from aging, maintenance neglect, and safety risks.

Bridges, water tanks, industrial plants, and multi-story buildings across India face critical challenges. Aging infrastructure combined with limited real-time monitoring creates significant safety risks and leads to expensive reactive repairs.

Maintenance teams often lack visibility into structural health, relying on periodic manual inspections that can miss early warning signs of failure. By the time issues are detected, the damage is often severe, costly to repair, and poses serious safety threats.

Key Challenges

  • Limited real-time visibility into infrastructure health
  • Reactive maintenance leading to disasters and expensive repairs
  • Manual inspection processes that miss early warning signs
  • Difficulty in compliance and regulatory reporting
  • Safety risks for personnel and the public

Technology Stack

Technology & Innovation

Deep-tech integration across hardware, software, and AI.

IoT Sensors & Hardware

Modular sensor nodes measuring vibration, stress/strain, load, corrosion, environmental conditions, and tilt. Custom sensor modules designed for harsh environments.

  • IoT Sensors & Hardware

    Modular sensor nodes measuring vibration, stress/strain, load, corrosion, environmental conditions, and tilt. Custom sensor modules designed for harsh environments.

  • Edge Computing & Cloud

    Distributed architecture with edge processing for real-time analysis and cloud storage for historical data. Secure data transmission with offline buffering.

  • AI & Machine Learning

    Anomaly detection algorithms trained on infrastructure behavior patterns. Predictive models for failure prediction and risk scoring with continuous learning.

  • Data Analytics & Visualization

    Time-series data processing with real-time dashboards showing infrastructure health. Trend analysis, pattern recognition, and exportable reports.

  • Computer Vision Integration

    Optional drone-based periodic image inspection with computer vision for hard-to-access structures. Sensor fusion combining image data with physical measurements.
