Product

InfraGuardAI

A hardware + software + AI-powered platform for real-time structural health monitoring and safety management of critical infrastructure.

The Problem

Infrastructure in India suffers from aging, maintenance neglect, and safety risks.

Bridges, water tanks, industrial plants, and multi-story buildings across India face critical challenges. Aging infrastructure combined with limited real-time monitoring creates significant safety risks and leads to expensive reactive repairs.

Maintenance teams often lack visibility into structural health, relying on periodic manual inspections that can miss early warning signs of failure. By the time issues are detected, the damage is often severe, costly to repair, and poses serious safety threats.

Key Challenges

  • Limited real-time visibility into infrastructure health
  • Reactive maintenance leading to disasters and expensive repairs
  • Manual inspection processes that miss early warning signs
  • Difficulty in compliance and regulatory reporting
  • Safety risks for personnel and the public

The Solution

InfraGuardAI provides continuous, intelligent monitoring of critical infrastructure.

Real-Time Monitoring

Continuous data collection from IoT sensors tracking vibration, stress, load, corrosion, and environmental factors.

AI-Driven Predictive Maintenance

Machine learning models detect anomalies, predict potential failures, and generate proactive maintenance alerts.

Safety Alerts & Risk Scoring

Automated risk assessment with instant alerts for critical conditions, enabling rapid response to potential issues.

Historical Analytics

Track trends over time, understand degradation patterns, and make data-driven maintenance decisions.

Compliance & Reporting

Automated reporting for regulatory compliance with exportable data and audit trails.

Dashboard & Mobile Access

Web and mobile interfaces for facility managers and engineers to monitor status from anywhere.

Technology & Innovation

Deep-tech integration across hardware, software, and AI.

  • IoT Sensors & Hardware

    Modular sensor nodes measuring vibration, stress/strain, load, corrosion, environmental conditions, and tilt. Custom sensor modules designed for harsh environments with data acquisition and transmission capabilities.

  • Edge Computing & Cloud

    Distributed architecture with edge processing for real-time analysis and cloud storage for historical data. Secure data transmission with offline buffering and failover mechanisms.

  • AI & Machine Learning

    Anomaly detection algorithms trained on infrastructure behavior patterns. Predictive models for failure prediction and risk scoring. Continuous learning from new data.

  • Data Analytics & Visualization

    Time-series data processing with real-time dashboards showing infrastructure health. Trend analysis, pattern recognition, and exportable reports.

  • Optional Computer Vision Integration

    Drone-based periodic image inspection with computer vision for hard-to-access structures. Sensor fusion combining image data with physical measurements.
